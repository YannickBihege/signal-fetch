## Framework

The Signal evaluates positions not in nominal currency terms, but relative to gold — the monetary baseline against which all assets are measured. Each holding is expressed in troy ounces of gold, stripping away the distortions of monetary inflation and currency fluctuation. A position is only worth accumulating if it is becoming *more* valuable in real, gold-denominated terms.

Momentum is assessed using the 14-period Relative Strength Index (RSI-14). Entry and sizing decisions follow a disciplined, rules-based overlay:

| RSI-14 Range | Signal |
|---|---|
| RSI < 40 | **BUY** — oversold relative to recent momentum |
| RSI 40–70 | **HOLD** — momentum neutral; maintain existing positions |
| RSI > 70 | **NO ENTRY** — overbought; do not add; review stop levels |

No new capital is deployed into overbought positions, regardless of the narrative surrounding them. Conviction is expressed through patience, not momentum-chasing.
