## Framework

The Signal evaluates positions not in nominal currency terms but relative to gold — the portfolio's benchmark and unit of account. Each holding is sized and tracked in troy ounces of gold, stripping away the noise of fiat debasement and isolating real purchasing-power performance. A position that rises in USD but falls in gold oz is, by this measure, losing ground.

Momentum is monitored via the 14-period Relative Strength Index (RSI-14) to avoid chasing overbought entries or ignoring deteriorating setups. The rules are simple:

| RSI-14 | Signal |
|---|---|
| < 40 | **BUY** — oversold relative to recent range |
| 40 – 70 | **HOLD** — momentum neutral to constructive |
| > 70 | **NO ENTRY** — overbought; avoid new capital deployment |

Existing holders in a NO ENTRY zone are not required to sell; the signal applies to fresh capital only.
